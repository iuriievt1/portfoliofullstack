Sentences

Client, Sentences

On 23 November 2024, the premiere of SENTENCES - a wordless immersive performance, first of its kind in the Czech Republic and Europe - took place at the UNESCO Kaiserstein Palace in Prague. The performance introduced a new artistic perspective, exploring the paradoxes and contradictions of the modern world, the topics people think about but rarely discuss openly.

The story followed a protagonist moving through symbolic rooms, confronting social norms, class divides, rejection, and isolation. Every movement is part of a space, each scene is a separate room with its own theme, mood, and meaning. The event ran across five time slots between 17:00 and 22:00, each limited to 20–25 guests to preserve intimacy, with a total capacity of 150. The evening began with a lounge reception at 17:00 featuring refreshments, DJs, and entertainment from the hosts, offering guests a relaxed social space before and after the performance.

VOGUE

Client, VOGUE

@hahluskha for VOGUE - is a young Ukrainian art director, performative artist, and photographer who came to the Czech Republic after the war limited her ability to create. Known for merging fashion with social topics, she gained attention during Paris Fashion Week, where she appeared outside the Rick Owens and Acne Studios shows wearing a collaborative piece with Czech designer David Altner - a light skirt-and-hoodie set with the message “Write anything on me.”

The performance, which led to security escorting her away from one of the venues, highlighted the impact of her work and quickly spread online. For this project, our cameraman and photographer created the full visual concept and captured the key moments that shaped the narrative. Their work became the foundation of the story published in Vogue, presenting Hahlushka’s creative statement and its context through strong, documentary-style imagery.

Automyčka Karlín

Client, Automyčka Karlín

Automyčka Karlín is a car-wash service located in Prague’s Karlín district. Unlike automated drive-through washes, it offers hand washing, ensuring gentler treatment of your car’s paint and a detailed interior clean. The service caters to drivers who value careful, attentive care over quick, impersonal washes.

Earlier in 2025, Automyčka Karlín got a new owner, and we led a full rebranding and brand-identity overhaul. The new visual identity, refreshed messaging, and updated brand presence reflect a modern, professional, and customer-focused approach, emphasizing quality service and a welcoming experience for every client.

Client, European League of Football
